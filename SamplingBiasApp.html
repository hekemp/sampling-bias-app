Random Sampling with Bias


In this app, you will draw a random sample from the population of all Galoots on Planet Z.

A Galoot

Step 1: Choose the variable you want to graph:

Weight Cares for Offspring

Step 2: Choose the sample size you want to use:

10 40 160
Step 3: Explore the effects of sampling bias by choosing a bias option:
Note: Changing the settings in step 2 or step 3 will clear all previous results.

Step 4: When you click on "Draw a Sample" the program will randomly select n galoots from the population.
 
Step 5: Click "Store result" to write the sample mean to the data table in the lower left. This also adds the sample mean to the empirical sampling distribution on the lower right.

Note: Steps 4 and 5 can be repeated as many times as you like. The more data you add to the empirical sampling distribution, the more it will accurately represent the theoretical sampling distribution for the sample mean.

Anytime you want to start over you can either click on "Clear all data" or change either the variable or sample size.

No Sampling Bias
All galoots on Planet Z have an equal chance for being chosen for a random sample.
